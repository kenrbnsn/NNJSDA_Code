AL'e'MO Squares of Brooklyn is the caring and sharing square dance club.

We are enthusiastic members of the Northern NJ Square Dancers Association.

The Al “e” Mo Square Dance Club has been dancing in Brooklyn for 34 years.   We offer lessons to all age groups on Wednesdays starting on October 3, 2012 at 7:00 at Saints Simon & Jude Lower Church, Avenue T between Lake and Van Sicklen Streets.  Regular classes for members start on September 12th.  

We have changed our Sunday dance schedule and we now start at 3 PM beginning on September 23rd.  We will dance to various callers this year and have many events planned.     So why not join Al “e” Mo for an afternoon of fun.
